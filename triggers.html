COMMON TRIGGERS

Triggers are mini-algorithms that are commonly used. Naming them can make it easier to learn the algorithms that use them.

Trigger Name
RUR'URU2R' Sune ("soo-nay")
R'U'RU'R'U2R Anti-Sune
RUR'U' Sexy Move
L'U'LU Sexy Move mirrored
R'FRF' Sledgehammer
LF'L'F Sledgehammer mirrored
FR'F'R Reversed Sledgehammer, a.k.a. Hedgeslammer
RUR'F' Lasso*
R'FR2U' Rebound*
RUR'U Half Sune
R'U'RU' Half Anti-Sune
*My own label.

These can help you use a memorization technique called chunking. For example, a Y-Permutation from PLL...

FRU'R'U'RUR'F'RUR'U'R'FRF'

...is 17 pieces of information. But when you think in terms of triggers, it becomes just 8 or even 5 pieces of information to remember:

F (RU'R'U') Lasso Sexy Sledgehammer
