3x3 CFOP Tutorial

PLL permutes the last layer corners and edges in one algorithm. There is nothing intuitive about PLL. You recognize which case you have, and you execute the algorithm for it as fluidly and quickly as you can. Sound familiar?

2-look PLL has only 6 algorithms. I have flashcards for them. My guide to the 4-look last layer also includes 2-look PLL.

Full PLL has 21 cases. I have flashcards for all them, and my 3-look last layer guide has all of them on one page.

Tips

The tips I gave for OLL also apply to PLL. Learn to recognize the case immediately, but AUF if needed.

There are many possible algorithms for each case, so please explore to find the ones you like best. There are good lists of them here and here.

Once you become familiar with CFOP, your journey is far from over. There are many supplements, variants, and alternate methods which await.
