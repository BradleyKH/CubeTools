3x3 Roux Tutorial

I am not a Roux expert, but I can point you to those who are.

Kian Mansour has achieved sub-7 averages with the Roux method. His homepage and YouTube channel have great resources to help you progress in Roux.

Some advanced concepts in Roux include:

  • Build the blocks in a different order. Sometimes the center is last!
  • Influencing the second block while building the first.
  • Build the two blocks simultaneously.
  • EOLR: solve edge orientation while setting up UL/UR. Kian has a nice tutorial on this, and Gilles Roux's tutorial also deals with it.

Happy cubing!
